Frequently Asked Questions

LifeLink is a platform that helps hospitals find organs for patients in need. Donors register with consent and health details; hospitals can then locate nearby hospitals with organs in stock and access donor information when authorised.

Any adult who wishes to donate organs can register through our consent form. You will need to provide basic details and your health history. Eligibility for actual donation is determined by medical teams at the time, in line with national guidelines.

No. Donor details, availability checks, and organ donor information are only accessible to authorised hospitals. The public can use the map to see where hospitals with organs in stock are located, and can register as donors or make donations.

The Live Organ page shows a map of nearby hospitals that have organs in stock. It also has options to check organ donor details (for hospitals), blood donor details, register as a donor (consent form), and to support through donations.

Donor information is stored and shared only for the purpose of organ matching. Access to full donor and health details is restricted to hospitals that are part of the system. We follow applicable privacy and data protection standards.
